Fallout: New Vegas - Character Creation guide

Fallout: New Vegas is a classic game preceding the success of Fallout 3. A majority of New Vegas is built off of Fallout 3’s engine and game aspects. Creating your character in New Vegas shares some similarities such as “SPECIAL” and skills, while also adding a new mechanic; traits. While you may be confused about how to use these elements, hopefully this guide can give some clarity of how to utilize them.

After creating your character’s appearance in Fallout: New Vegas, you will be soon introduced to your character's SPECIAL stats. Each letter represents an attribute about your character, and symbolizing these characteristics:

SPECIAL Stat Skills Affected Additional Effects
Strength Melee weapons, Unarmed Melee damage, Carry weight, Weapon effectiveness
Perception Energy weapons, Explosives, Lockpick Enemy detection, Certain dialog options with NPC’s
Endurance Survival Hit points, Passive healing, Elemental resistance (Poison / Radiation)
Charisma Barter, Speech Companions armor and damage
Intelligence Medicine, Repair, Science Skill points gained per level up
Agility Guns, Sneak V.A.T.S. Action points, draw/holster speed, reload speed.
Luck All Skills Critical strike chance, Casino odds
